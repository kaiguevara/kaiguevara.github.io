About Me

I’m driven by a passion to tackle some of the biggest challenges facing our planet, especially in how we produce, use, and think about energy. My work sits at the intersection of cutting-edge technology, sustainability, and climate resilience. From designing smarter energy systems to exploring innovative solutions, I’m all about transforming ideas into impact.

I dive deep into the tools that help illuminate new paths forward. Whether it’s modeling energy patterns, dissecting policy implications, or developing technology for a cleaner future, my projects reflect a relentless curiosity and commitment to real-world results.

This site is where I share those projects, insights, and the evolving journey I’m on to innovate in energy and drive change. If you’re interested in sustainable energy, climate solutions, or just cool tech, you’re in the right spot.

Let’s explore the future of energy together.

Mission

To drive meaningful change in how energy systems operate by leveraging technology, data, and innovative thinking. I aim to accelerate the transition toward resilient, sustainable, and equitable energy solutions that can adapt to our evolving climate challenges.

Approach

My approach is deeply interdisciplinary, blending economics, policy, finance, data science, technology, and innovation. By integrating insights from these diverse fields, I develop holistic solutions that address complex energy challenges from multiple angles, fostering impactful progress.

Focus

I focus on serving communities through advancing climate resilience and equity. I strive to improve the tools we have, using AI to enhance energy capabilities and leveraging market incentives to transform the energy system’s capacity for economic growth and climate resilience.
