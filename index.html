Mission

I believe in using data, design, and collaboration to accelerate the clean energy transition. My work focuses on resilience, equity, and creating practical tools that make decarbonization accessible for everyone.

About Me

I studied Environmental Science at UC Berkeley and Data Science at the graduate level. My experience includes designing microgrids in Death Valley, building home decarbonization software, and creating tools to support climate risk analysis and resilience planning.

Projects

  • ArcGIS StoryMaps – Visual analyses of urban air quality, redlining, and environmental justice in the Bay Area.
